Welcome to the MCU Guide!

This website was created to answer some common questions: how am I supposed to watch Marvel movies when there are dozens of them and they all build on each other? Do I have to go all the way back to the very beginning and watch them all? What order should I watch them in? Are the TV shows a part of this too? And what's this about a "multiverse"?

On this site, we catalogue all the Marvel Cinematic Universe films and series. You'll see which ones are great places for a newcomer to jump into the universe, and which ones require a bit of preparation to fully understand. Every time you watch a new film or TV show, you can come here and enter it on our checklist, and we'll give you new recommendations.
